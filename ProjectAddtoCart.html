Rice

Rice is a cereal crop belonging to the family Poecae. Rice being a tropical crop can be grown during the two distinct seasons (dry and wet) of the year provided that moisture is made available to the crop. Rice is commonly consumed as food around the world. Both white and brown rice contain mainly carbohydrate and some protein, with virtually no fat or sugar
